Refunds

General Refund Policy

Eligibility Refunds may be issued at the discretion of Easy Trips for eligible cancellations. Non-Refundable Services: Some services, such as certain promotions, discounted tickets, or last-minute bookings, may be non-refundable. Please refer to the specific terms and conditions for each service at the time of booking. Change Fees: Changes to bookings may incur a change fee, even if a full refund is not granted.

Bus Ticket Refunds

Cancellation: Refunds may be granted for cancellations made within 5 hours of the original booking time. Note that Refunds may be restricted or unavailable for certain bus companies or ticket types. No-Shows: No refunds will be issued for passengers who fail to board the bus at the scheduled time and date.

Plane Ticket Refunds

Cancellation: Refunds for plane tickets will generally be subject to the specific refund policies of the airline operating the flight. Easy Trips will assist in processing refund requests with the airline, but the final decision on refunds rests with the airline. No-Shows: No refunds will be issued for passengers who miss their scheduled flight.

Car Ride Refunds

Cancellation: Refunds may be granted for cancellations made within 24 hours of the scheduled pickup time. A cancellation fee may apply for late cancellations or no-shows. No-Shows: A no-show fee may be charged if the passenger is not present at the scheduled pickup location within [Number] minutes of the scheduled time.

Processing

Refund Method: Refunds will generally be issued to the original form of payment. Processing Time: Refunds may take up to 5 business days to process.

Delays Policy

Easy Trips: Delays Policy

Understanding Possible Delays At Easy Trips, we strive to provide punctual and reliable transportation services. However, unforeseen circumstances can sometimes lead to delays in bus, plane, or car rides.

Factors that may cause delays:

  • Traffic: Heavy traffic, accidents, and road closures can significantly impact travel times, especially in urban areas.
  • Weather: Adverse weather conditions, such as heavy rain, fog, or snow, can cause delays or even service disruptions.
  • Mechanical Issues: Technical problems with vehicles (buses, planes, cars) may require maintenance or repairs, leading to delays.
  • Air Traffic Control: Delays at airports due to air traffic congestion or other operational issues.
  • Unexpected Events: Unforeseen events such as accidents, natural disasters, or security incidents can cause significant disruptions.
  • Legal Considerations:

Brazilian law allows for a certain degree of flexibility regarding transportation delays. Specific regulations may vary depending on the mode of transport:

  • Buses: Intercity bus companies are generally allowed a reasonable delay due to unforeseen circumstances.
  • Planes: Airlines are subject to regulations by the National Civil Aviation Agency (ANAC) regarding flight delays and passenger rights.
  • Car Rides: While specific regulations may vary, delays for car rides are generally acceptable within reasonable limits.

Easy Trips Commitment:

  • Transparency: We will do our best to inform passengers of any expected delays as soon as possible.
  • Communication: We will provide regular updates on the status of delayed services.
  • Assistance: We will assist passengers with rebooking options or alternative transportation arrangements whenever possible.
